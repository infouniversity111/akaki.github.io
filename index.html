Study at Akaki Tsereteli State University

Akaki Tsereteli State University is a multilevel higher education institution that performs academic and professional programs and is engaged in active research activities

It provides the provision of life-long continuing education programs and services, generation and distribution of new knowledge and together with various stakeholders is involved in the formation of public policy.

The University, based on the traditions of the Pazisi and Gelati Academies, the principles of "Great Charter of Universities"and the national and European standards for higher education
