Requirements for the Degree

MBBS, or Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS full form), is a Six-year undergraduate medical degree programme from Georgia

  • Applicants must have completed at least Class 12
  • Applicants must have at least 50% in the 12th
  • Applicants must pass NEET
  • The minimum percentage for reserved category is 40%.
  • 12th in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • candidates must be at least 17 years old
